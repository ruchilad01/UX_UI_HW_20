TripEase

Seamless Journeys, Effortlessly Planned.

People find it challenging to make travel bookings separately on various apps, finding it time-consuming and tricky to manage within their budget. TripEase simplifies by consolidating travel bookings under one roof.

My Role:
UX/UI Designer
Tools:
Figma, Miro, Invision, Illustrator
Timeframe:
3 Weeks

Background

After the pandemic, people have resumed traveling to countries as they did before. The research aim is to identify the challenges people face when planning their trips post-pandemic. The primary objective is to recognize their pain points and create an app that simplifies travelersâ€™ experiences by consolidating their needs in one place.

Goal

To develop an app that simplifies the travel planning process for users. This app will incorporate all travel-related aspects in one place. Users can simplify input their budget and preferences, and the app will provide booking suggestions within their budget, eliminating the need to separately book flights and accommodations. Additionally, the app will generate a comprehensive trip itinerary.

Methodology

User interviews will be conducted to collect qualitative data from participants. These Interviews will involve asking 5-6 users questions related to budget, destinations and bookings. The data collection process will take approximately 3 days and will help to build a better understanding of the user persona.
